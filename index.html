About Us

OMAID is an NGO established in 2018 in Kabul, Afghanistan. Our Vision is a Stable Afghanistan, based on participatory development and consensus on community level, whereby discriminations of all sorts are brought to the minimum. Our Mission is To do peacebuilding by engaging youth, women, community elders and religious scholars in resolving conflicts, social cohesion challenges on community level and scale up the process to create peaceful co-existence consensus between different ethnicities, clans, languages and religions across Afghanistan. Also we want to implement Area-based development by actively engaging communities in all phases of the programs and Currently we are focusing on the demand driven emergency relief programs.

Focused Areas

  • Strengthen peace building and social cohesion on grass root level by dispute resolution programs.
  • Area Based- Development and Resilience Building programs.
  • Community based Participatory needs assessment, design and implementation of programs.
  • Improve Monitoring and Evaluation mechanisms while implementation of programs.
  • Emergency relief and job-creation projects.

Values

  • Transparency
  • Accountability
  • Comprehensive and active community engagement from all genders and ages I.e., youth, women, community elders and religious scholars in design, M&E and implementation of programs .
  • Gender-Mainstreaming.
  • Demand Driven and Community Driven programs design, and implementation.
